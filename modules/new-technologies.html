MODULE 1

INTRODUCTION TO NEW TECHNOLOGIES



Welcome to the New Jersey State Innovation Skills Accelerator. Launched by Governor Phil Murphy, the Innovation Skills Accelerator is a free, online program to train public officials to leverage new technology, data, collective intelligence and design thinking to allow our State’s public servants to advance the way we solve public problems. Each training module includes video instruction, interviews with leading public sector innovators from around the world, readings and self-assessments. Explore below to get started today!
