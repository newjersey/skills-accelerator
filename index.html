BECOMING A PUBLIC ENTREPRENEUR

INNOVATION SKILLS ACCELERATOR



Welcome to the New Jersey State Innovation Skills Accelerator. The Innovation Skills Accelerator is a free, online program, complemented by live training events, to train public entrepreneurs to use innovative methods, including design thinking, evidence-based decisionmaking and collective intelligence, to solve public problems.
Each module of the online training includes video instruction, interviews with leading public sector innovators from around the world, readings and self-assessments.
Live bootcamps and workshops provide opportunities for participants to learn more about how to apply those skills to their work in government.
